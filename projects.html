    computer-assisted drug discovery: a review 2022

    Computer-assistance in drug discovery has come a long way since its inception in the 90s. As technological capabilities and techniques advance, so to do the capabilities of computer assistance in drug discovery, now capable of nearly automating several phases of the drug discovery pipeline. Several methods and implementations are discussed, evaluating their metrics, advantages, and disadvantages, including virtual screening, traditional machine learning, and machine learning based on chemical structure. full paper.

    face image translation using CycleGAN 2022

    adjust the features of an image through the application of various "filters", each a pretrained CycleGAN model. for example, add glasses to your photo, or a double chin, or make your lips look a little bigger. trained off of annotated CelebA. live link coming soon. learn more about CycleGANs.

    hormone detection from MRI brain scans using volumetric convolutional model 2021

    predicted the status of a genetic biomarker important for brain cancer treatment through MRI scans of brain tumor patients using volumetric (3D) convolutional model. more info on kaggle.

    VR driving simulator 2021-2022

    developed VR Driving simulator in Unity for use in research projects at Chapman Brain Institute, using ForceSeat SDK for controlling racing chair capable of 6 degrees of motion.
